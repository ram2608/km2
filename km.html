About Us!

While Katha means story, and Munch means stage, we bring both together for our lovely audience to enjoy the creations of tales that we put together. We are a very high performing acting organization located in the SFO Bay Area.

Our Next Play!

Our next play is called "Arabian Nights." The original Arabian Nights date back to the times of the Abbasid Empire, with it's capital situated in present-day Baghdad. We bring to you variations of these tales, while adding in comedy, gossip, romance, and much more!
